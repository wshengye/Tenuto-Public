Privacy Policy

Effective date: 7 June 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how DigiPetSG, a sole proprietorship registered in Singapore operating the Tenuto application (“Tenuto”, “we”, “us”), handles personal data, in line with the Personal Data Protection Act 2012 (“PDPA”). By using Tenuto you agree to this policy.

Two kinds of data, two roles

Tenuto handles two distinct categories of personal data:

(a) Your account data. Data about you, the Advisor — your name, email, business name, settings, and usage. For this data, we are the organisation responsible under the PDPA.

(b) Client Data. Data you enter about your clients and prospects. For this data, you are the organisation with the relationship to those individuals and responsible for obtaining their consent and giving them any required notification; we act only as your data intermediary, processing Client Data on your behalf and on your instructions, and not for our own purposes.

What we collect

Account data: name, email address, business name, authentication identifiers (including from Apple or Google sign-in), subscription status, app settings, and usage/diagnostic data.

Client Data you input or import: client/prospect names, contact details, dates of birth, relationships and family details, notes, appointments, follow-ups, and financial information such as income, budget, and insurance policy details. Sensitive financial fields (e.g. income, budget, policy numbers, premiums, coverage amounts, and your private notes) are encrypted at rest.

How we use data

We use account data to provide, secure, and improve the service and to communicate with you. We process Client Data only to provide the features you use — storing your records, generating follow-ups, and drafting messages at your request. We do not sell personal data, and we do not use Client Data for our own marketing or to train AI models.

AI drafting

When you ask Tenuto to draft a message, a limited set of data — the client’s first name, persona, the private notes you wrote for that client, and your writing-style profile — is sent to Google’s Gemini API to generate text. We do not send full contact lists, phone numbers, or identifiers to the model. The AI provider processes this data to return a draft; the API key remains on our server.

Disclosure and processors

We share data only with service providers that help us run Tenuto — our hosting and database provider (Supabase) and the AI provider (Google) — under terms that require them to protect it, and where required by law. We do not otherwise disclose your data.

Overseas transfer

Our service providers may store or process data on servers outside Singapore. Where personal data is transferred overseas, we take reasonable steps to ensure a standard of protection comparable to the PDPA.

Security

We protect data with encryption in transit (TLS), encryption at rest for sensitive financial fields, row-level access controls that isolate each Advisor’s data, and server-side handling of secrets. No system is perfectly secure, but we take reasonable measures appropriate to the sensitivity of the data.

Retention and deletion

We keep data for as long as your account is active. You can permanently delete your account and all associated data at any time from the app’s Settings; deletion is immediate and irreversible. As our data intermediary role is on your instructions, you are responsible for the retention and deletion obligations you owe your own clients.

Your rights under the PDPA

You may request access to, or correction of, your account data, and you may withdraw consent (which may mean you can no longer use Tenuto). For Client Data, requests from your clients should be directed to you as the responsible organisation; we will assist you as your intermediary. Contact us at digipetsg@gmail.com.

Children

Tenuto is a tool for financial-advisory professionals and is not directed at children. Do not create an account if you are under 18.

Data breaches

If a data breach likely to result in significant harm occurs, we will take reasonable steps to address it and to notify affected parties and the Personal Data Protection Commission as required by law.

Changes

We may update this policy; material changes will be notified in-app or by email.

Contact

Questions, access/correction requests, or to withdraw consent: digipetsg@gmail.com.
