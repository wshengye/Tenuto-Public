What we store

Your account, the contacts you add or import, appointments, follow-ups, and the messages you draft. All rows are scoped to your account by row-level security — other advisors cannot read your data.

AI drafting

When you draft a message, the contact’s first name, persona, your private notes for that contact, and your writing-style profile are sent to Google’s Gemini API to generate the text. The API key never leaves our server. We do not send full contact lists or phone numbers to the model.

WhatsApp

Messages are opened in WhatsApp via a deep link with the text pre-filled. We never send messages on your behalf; you review and tap send yourself.

Calendar

With your permission, the app reads your device calendar’s busy times to propose open slots and writes appointments you schedule. Calendar data stays on your device.
