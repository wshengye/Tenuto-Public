Important — please read. This tool produces illustrative estimates only and is not financial advice, an offer, or a guarantee of any payout. Figures use 2026 CPF parameters (contribution & allocation rates, $8,000 monthly wage ceiling, 2.5% OA / 4% SMRA interest with extra-interest tiers, BRS $110,200 · FRS $220,400 · ERS $440,800) and assume contributions continue to age 55, after which balances grow at CPF interest with no further contributions or withdrawals. CPF LIFE payouts are modelled from CPF Board's published Standard-Plan estimates (male basis, ~7% female adjustment) and interpolated; Basic and Escalating figures are approximations. Actual CPF rules, interest rates, retirement sums, and payouts are set by the CPF Board and change over time. Verify any figure with the official CPF LIFE Estimator and a licensed adviser before acting.
